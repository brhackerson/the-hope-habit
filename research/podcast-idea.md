## Format Options

**Solo format** — Brian reading and reflecting on posts, with added context that doesn't make it into the writing. The star wall. The two-hour drive spoken aloud. The golf story in his own voice. Intimate and personal. Low production barrier. Easiest to launch.

**Interview format** — conversations with people in the trusted circle and the like-hearted community. Michaele on building ABS. Jim on witnessing the dumbbell moment. The pastor friend on hope and faith. Dr. Lee on the biology. Guests who have lived adjacent to the practice.

**Hybrid** — solo episodes anchored to posts, plus occasional guest conversations that deepen the themes.

## Natural First Episode

The two-hour drive. The decision. What the inner critic said. What answered it. The origin story of the entire practice — told in audio for the first time. Intimate, honest, and complete. No guest needed. Just the story.

---

## Connection to the Book

The podcast and the book can grow together. Early episodes could test which stories land with listeners before committing them to the manuscript. Listener responses could surface questions and angles that haven't been considered yet. The podcast is also a marketing platform for the book when it publishes.
